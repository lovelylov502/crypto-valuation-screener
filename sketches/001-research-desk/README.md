## Variant: Research Desk

### Design stance
A calm analyst desk where reasoning and next research questions are more important than raw data density.

### Key choices
- Layout: left navigation, KPI strip, candidate cards, right-side question panel.
- Typography: dark professional UI with readable Korean body text.
- Color: muted dark base with green/amber/red reserved for decision states.
- Interaction: clicking a card opens a detail drawer; Escape closes it.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: serious decision-making, reducing dashboard clutter, making questions visible.
- Weak at: showing many candidates at once.

### Best for
태규가 매일 “오늘 뭘 볼까?”를 판단하는 default home screen.
