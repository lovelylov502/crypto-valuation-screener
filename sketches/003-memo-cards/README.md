## Variant: Memo Cards

### Design stance
An Obsidian/Notion-like research board where each token is a thesis memo, not just a row.

### Key choices
- Layout: kanban-style columns by decision bucket.
- Typography: paper-like editorial feel for research notes.
- Color: warm paper background; subtle decision tags.
- Interaction: clicking a memo opens a detail modal with checklist and next action.

### Trade-offs
- Strong at: turning candidates into research work items and preserving reasoning.
- Weak at: less efficient for scanning hundreds of names.

### Best for
Candidate queue and research workflow, especially if later connected to wiki/Obsidian.
